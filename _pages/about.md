Welcome to my academic world! I am a forest ecologist interested in how environmental variability influences plant function and forest processes. My work integrates field and lab research, statistical modelling, and advanced technologies such as RGB imaging, LiDAR, and hyperspectral data to study ecological patterns. I am particularly interested in forest carbon dynamics and modeling, and I enjoy working with data to better understand these systems.
